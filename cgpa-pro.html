Privacy Policy for CGPA Pro

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

CGPA Pro (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices when using our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information you enter in the app

Academic inputs such as marks/grades, selected university/scheme/course/semester, and calculated SGPA/CGPA results. This data is stored locally on your device for app functionality.

b) Device and usage data (via third-party services)

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interaction and ad-related events
  • Diagnostic/performance information

c) Purchase-related data

If you buy “remove ads” or similar features, payment handling is done by Google Play. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

  • Calculate and display academic results
  • Save results locally
  • Show ads (unless removed via purchase)
  • Validate in-app purchases
  • Improve app performance

3. Data Storage

Most academic data you enter is stored locally on your device. We do not require account signup and we do not sell personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (advertisements)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

5. Permissions

  • Storage access to save result images (only when you choose to save)
  • Internet access for ads and purchase verification

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your academic data except when required to provide ads/purchase services or if required by law.

7. Data Retention

Data saved in the app remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13.

9. Your Choices

  • Stop using the app anytime
  • Clear app data from device settings
  • Uninstall the app

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the Effective Date.
