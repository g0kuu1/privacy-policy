Privacy Policy for Readable: RSVP Speed Reading

Effective date: March 18, 2026

Readable: RSVP Speed Reading ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Handle

a) Content you provide in the app

  • Text you paste into the app
  • PDF files you choose to import
  • URLs you paste for article extraction

This content is used to provide the reading experience (tokenization, display, and session processing).

b) App usage and settings stored locally

The app stores certain data on your device (local storage), such as:

  • Reading settings (for example selected speed, dark mode, reminders)
  • Progress and streak information
  • Ad-free/pro unlock state

c) Purchase information

If you buy ad-free access, purchase status is processed via Google Play Billing to unlock paid features.

d) Advertising-related data (via Google AdMob)

The app displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect data such as device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads, subject to Google policies.

2. How We Use Information

We use handled information to:

  • Run core app features (speed reading sessions)
  • Let you import and read your own text/PDF/URL content
  • Show ads (unless you purchased ad-free)
  • Process and restore purchases
  • Schedule optional local reminder notifications

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may process data under their own privacy terms:

  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

Please review their policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Terms

4. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or device settings.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Most app data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate our own backend server for user accounts or cloud profiles in this app version.
  • Data you provide (text/PDF/URL-derived text) is processed for app functionality.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address the issue.

7. Your Choices

  • Avoid optional features (for example URL import)
  • Disable notifications
  • Purchase ad-free to remove ads
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device

8. International Users

Depending on your region, advertising and platform providers may process data in countries other than your own under their legal frameworks.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a new effective date.
